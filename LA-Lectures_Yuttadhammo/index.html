The Los Angeles Lectures

By Bhante Yuttadhammo

A series of Dhamma talks delivered to serious beginners over the course of a few months in 2009. The talks give a penetrating introduction to the essence of the teachings and are recommended for all.

I have reordered and renumbered the original lectures here according to the chapters in Buddhist Life, Buddhist Path which they best correspond with, creating a more gradual and sensible order to the lecture series than you’ll find online. It’s therefore recommended that you listen to them in the order provided below or on Google Drive, rather than listening chronologically.
